Shipping & Returns

Shipping Policy

We are committed to delivering your order accurately, in good condition, and always on time.

  • We ship nationwide across Pakistan.
  • All orders are dispatched within 24-48 hours of confirmation.
  • You can expect your order to be delivered within 2-5 working days, depending on your city.
  • A tracking link will be sent to your WhatsApp or email address once your order is dispatched.
  • We offer a flat shipping rate on all orders.

Return Policy

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you are not, you can return the product to us.

  • Returns are accepted within 7 days of delivery.
  • To be eligible for a return, your item must be unused, in the original packaging, and in the same condition that you received it.
  • To initiate a return, please contact our customer support team via WhatsApp or the contact form on our website with your order details.
  • Once your return is received and inspected, we will notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund.
  • If approved, your refund will be processed via bank transfer, JazzCash, or Easypaisa.

Warranty

All watches come with a 1-year warranty covering the machine/movement. This warranty does not cover damage from misuse, accidents, or normal wear and tear on the strap, glass, or case.
