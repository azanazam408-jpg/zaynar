Frequently Asked Questions

Payment

What payment methods do you accept?
We accept JazzCash, Easypaisa, cash on delivery, and secure card payments.
Is my payment information safe?
Yes, all payments are processed securely using industry-standard encryption.

Shipping

Where do you ship?
We ship nationwide across Pakistan using reliable courier partners.
How long does delivery take?
Delivery usually takes 2-5 working days, depending on your city.
Can I track my order?
Yes, after dispatch, a tracking link is sent to your WhatsApp or email.

Products & Authenticity

Are your products genuine?
Yes, all our watches, wallets, and accessories are 100% original and quality checked.
Do you offer product warranty?
Most items include a 1-year warranty on the watch movement. See individual item descriptions or our Shipping & Returns page for warranty info.

Returns & Refunds

Can I return or exchange items?
Yes! Returns are accepted within 7 days if items are unused and in original packaging. Please see our Shipping & Returns page for full details.
How do I start a return or refund?
Contact us on WhatsApp or via the contact form with your order details.
